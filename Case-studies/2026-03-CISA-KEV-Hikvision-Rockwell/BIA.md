Business Impact Analysis (BIA): Industrial & Surveillance Exploitation

1. Financial Impact (Quantified)

Production Downtime: Estimated at $45,000 per hour for the primary manufacturing facility (Rockwell Impact).

Regulatory Fines: Potential for $10,000/day fines if non-compliant with specific critical infrastructure protection standards (NERC-CIP/TSA directives).

Incident Response Cost: Estimated $150,000 for forensic cleanup and third-party validation post-compromise.

2. Operational Impact

Safety (Life-Safety): Potential for physical injury to staff if safety interlocks are bypassed via Rockwell controllers.

Security Breach: Loss of physical perimeter control; inability to audit who entered the facility via Hikvision cameras.

3. Legal & Reputation Impact

Contractual Breaches: Failure to meet "Commercially Reasonable Security" clauses in customer SLAs.

Public Trust: A breach of surveillance footage would result in significant negative press and loss of investor confidence.

4. Recovery Time Objective (RTO)

Target RTO: 4 Hours (Recovery of control logic).

Target RPO: 24 Hours (Restore configuration from known-good backup).
