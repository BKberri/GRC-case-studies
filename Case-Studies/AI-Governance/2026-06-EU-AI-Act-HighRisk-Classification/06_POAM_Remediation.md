# Plan of Action & Milestones (POA&M) — Compliance Readiness Plan
## Case: EU AI Act — Draft High-Risk AI Classification Guidelines (Public Consultation)
**Risk ID:** RR-009 | **Opened:** 2026-06-08 | **Target Closure:** 2026-09-08 (90-day proactive readiness window)

| # | Action Item | Specific Task | Owner | Start | Due | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | AI system inventory | Compile a complete inventory of all AI/ML systems (internal LLM tools, HR/recruitment AI, customer-facing assistants, biometric/decision-support systems) accessible to or affecting EU-resident individuals | AI Governance Lead (Blaise Kingko) / Engineering | 2026-06-09 | 2026-06-23 | Open |

| 5 | Remediation scoping (conditional) | For any system classified as high-risk, scope the conformity-assessment, technical-documentation, and EU database registration work required ahead of the December 2027 milestone | AI Governance Lead / Legal / Engineering | Trigger-based | Within 30 days of classification | Contingent |
| 6 | Recurring review cadence | Establish a semi-annual AI-inventory and classification review tied to the GOVERN function, ensuring new AI deployments are classified at design time | AI Governance Lead (Blaise Kingko) | 2026-09-01 | 2026-09-08 | Open |

### Specific Reference Actions
- Submit consultation feedback via the European Commission's "Have Your Say" portal referencing the draft high-risk AI system classification guidelines (consultation closes **2026-06-23**)
- Use **NIST AI RMF Playbook MAP function guidance** as the working methodology for the inventory/classification exercise
- Cross-reference any system already subject to a GDPR Article 35 DPIA — reuse that evidence base rather than duplicating effort

### Escalation Trigger
If the inventory exercise (Action #1) identifies any AI system already in production that maps clearly to an Annex III high-risk category and lacks any existing impact-assessment documentation, escalate immediately to Legal & Regulatory Affairs and the executive AI governance sponsor — this converts the finding from "proactive readiness" to "active compliance gap" requiring expedited remediation scoping (Action #5) ahead of schedule.
