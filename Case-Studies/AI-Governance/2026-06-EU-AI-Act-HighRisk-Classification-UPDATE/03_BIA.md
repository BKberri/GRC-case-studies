# Business Impact Analysis — EU AI Act High-Risk Classification (UPDATE)

## Affected Business Function
AI governance program management, compliance roadmap planning, and board/executive reporting for organizations developing or deploying AI systems in scope of EU AI Act Annex III high-risk classification.

## Impact Categories

| Impact Type | Assessment |
|---|---|
| **Confidentiality** | Not applicable — this is a regulatory timeline change, not a technical vulnerability |
| **Integrity** | Low — governance documentation and roadmap artifacts should be updated to reflect revised deadlines |
| **Availability** | Not applicable |
| **Regulatory/Compliance** | Medium — revised deadline affects compliance program sequencing; ultimate obligation unchanged |
| **Reputational** | Low-Medium — organizations that publicly communicated compliance timelines may need to update external-facing statements |

## Recovery Time Objective (RTO) / Recovery Point Objective (RPO)
Not applicable in the technical sense. Recommend updating the AI governance program roadmap and board reporting materials within the current reporting cycle to reflect the revised timeline.

## Dependency Mapping
Any vendor contracts, customer commitments, or internal SLAs that referenced the original EU AI Act compliance deadline should be reviewed and updated to reflect the revised schedule, with care taken not to over-communicate timeline relief to stakeholders who may treat it as obligation relief.
