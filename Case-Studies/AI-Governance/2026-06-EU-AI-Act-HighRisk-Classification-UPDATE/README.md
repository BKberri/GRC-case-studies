Historical Pattern Recognition: Referencing GDPR's non-repeated two-year transition period as a basis for recommending continued program momentum despite the timeline relief — applying regulatory-history pattern recognition to a live compliance decision.

In-Place Risk Register Revision Over Duplication: This update revises existing Risk Register entries (RR-004, RR-009) rather than creating new rows, demonstrating proper dedup discipline for a regulatory development tied to a previously logged risk rather than a genuinely new finding.
